Workflow

General workflow

Development Seed's Data team has a structured and efficient workflow that involves the necessary processes and activities to generate a high-quality training dataset, and this helps them speed up the annotation process.

Once the Data team knows the requirements of the customer and has access to the data input, they start the development of the project based on their workflow that contains three main processes: data exploration & workflow, data annotation, and delivering data output. In each of these processes, different activities are developed until the training data is obtained.

Along the processes on the workflow, quality assurance process and a continuous communication were embedded to ensure the training data aligns with the customer's expectations.
